Types of AHA! links

In AHA! there are two types of links: links to a page and links to a concept.

Since in DM pages are linked to concepts AHA! can find out which concept corresponds to a page and which page corresponds to a concept.

Linking to a concept results in adaptive link destinations technique.

When a link is bound to a concept (rather than directly leading to a page) the adaptation engine uses a rule to determine which page to show, depending on the user model instance. A link to a concept may for instance lead to an introductory page for users who are missing some pre-requisite knowledge, whereas advanced users will see a more detailed description of the concept. Such adaptation is quite drastic, and most likely very obvious to the user.
