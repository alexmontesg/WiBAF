Introduction

Autism & Uni

Intro and context

Many students don't tell anybody at uni about their autism, not even the university itself. This makes it difficult for them to get the support they need, both officially and from their friends and the other people around them. At school or college, you might not have received or even needed any support outside your family, and the opportunity to reinvent yourself at uni means you don't have to tell anyone about your diagnosis. However, university is very different from school and college and the help available can level the playing field so you can concentrate on enjoying your time at uni and doing well on your course.
