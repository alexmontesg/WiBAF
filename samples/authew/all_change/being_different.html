Being Different

It's OK to be different but most people want someone that they can talk to and share interesrs with.

In fact, everyone you meet who is about the same age as you is GOING THROUGH THE SAME THING! In other words, how your body changes may seem to be unique BUT the feelings and changes that you are going through are similar to everyone else's.

So, why not chill out and think about others for a change. You never know, you may just find a friend by being sympathetic!

You may seem like an alien on the inside BUT you'd be surprised just how 'normal' you are!
