We change whatever age we are BUT as a teenager these changes are more dramatic! From height, weight and skin to temper, mood and the way that we see the world, and the way that the world sees us, everything changes! AND there is nothig that we can do about it. It's a natural process and part of growing up.

However, it does mean that your body sometimes seems to be out of control or conspiring against you! Don't worry: that's where The Teenage Years comes in so let the adventure begin!
